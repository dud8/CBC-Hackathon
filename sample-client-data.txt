Discovery Call Notes - TechStart Inc.
Date: November 9, 2025
Account Manager: Sarah Johnson

=== CLIENT INFORMATION ===

Company: TechStart Inc.
Website: techstart.io
Industry: B2B SaaS
Stage: Series B ($25M raised)

Product: AI-powered recruitment platform for tech companies
- Automates candidate sourcing and screening
- Uses ML to match candidates with job requirements
- Integrates with existing ATS systems (Greenhouse, Lever, etc.)
- Average time-to-hire reduced by 60% vs. traditional methods

=== TARGET AUDIENCE ===

Primary:
- Series B+ tech companies (100-1000 employees)
- HR Directors and VP of People

- Geographic focus: US (San Francisco, NYC, Austin, Seattle)

Secondary:
- Fast-growing startups (Series A)
- Technical recruiters at tech companies
